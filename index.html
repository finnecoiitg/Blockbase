About the Course

BlockBase 2025, brought to you by FEC is your all-access pass to the world of Blockchain, Web3, and DeFi.
This 7-day certified crash course is designed to take you from curious beginner to confident Web3 developer — no prior experience needed! 🌐
In this hands-on journey, you will:

  • 🔹 Understand the core principles of blockchain technology 🧱
  • 🔹 Explore Ethereum and set up your own crypto wallet 🔐
  • 🔹 Learn Solidity to write and deploy smart contracts 💻
  • 🔹 Build & launch your first decentralized app (dApp) 🚀
Whether you're here to explore or build the next big thing — you're in the right place. Let’s shape the future of the internet together ✨
